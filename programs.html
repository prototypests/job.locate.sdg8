Programs that are being implemented in differnet Local Government Units

    This initiative is specifically designed to support workers displaced from Internet Gaming Licensees (IGLs), formerly known as Philippine Offshore Gaming Operators (POGOs), ensuring they have access to alternative employment opportunities across Metro Manila. Displaced workers seeking new job opportunities will have another chance to secure employment as Project DAPAT: A Special DOLE Action Plan & Transition Job Fair for Displaced IGL Workers returns for its fifth run.

    The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) is a non-fee-charging, multi-employment service facility established within local government units (LGUs) across the Philippines. One of PESO's key initiatives is providing job fair schedules to facilitate employment opportunities. Its primary objective is to ensure the prompt and efficient delivery of employment facilitation services while providing timely information on labor market trends and Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE) programs.
